Who we are

This web application aims to address the issue of sexually transmitted diseases (STDs) by providing a platform for users to receive a diagnosis based on their symptoms and connect with an AI chatbot for further information. The application will offer information about various STDs, including their transmission methods and strategies to reduce the risk of infection. Additionally, the system will gather users' information regarding their past sexual behavior. The goal is to limit the spread of STDs and mitigate the complications associated with these diseases.
