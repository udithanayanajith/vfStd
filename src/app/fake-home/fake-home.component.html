search

  • What are sexually transmitted diseases (STDs)?

  • STDs/STIs are infections transmitted through sexual contact.

  • They can be spread during vaginal, oral, or anal sex, as well as through skin-to-skin contact

  • Common STDs include chlamydia, gonorrhea, syphilis, genital herpes, HPV, and HIV.

  • Safe sex practices, regular testing, and open communication with partners are important for prevention.

  • Some STDs can be treated, while others are lifelong conditions. Education and healthcare access are crucial.
